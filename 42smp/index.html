Clarification on the rules

Stealing

Stealing means taking items from, or breaking/destroying, any container in Minecraft that can hold items/blocks. E.g. Chests, barrels, furnaces, armour stands, brewing stands, etc. unless explicit permission from the owner is given.
Please refrain from opening containers without permission as this may make you look sus. Stealing from farms is NOT allowed (this includes killing mobs). Items lying on the ground are allowed to be picked up.

Griefing

Griefing means killing any mob (hostile, passive, or neutral) that is owned by another member. Griefing also means to break blocks in someones property/land and causing damage. Trapping is allowed, but without causing damage to someones property/land or mobs.
