What is Spike?

Spike is a kind and friendly cactus who throws cactus grenades at enemies!
He is a legendary brawler and was one of the originals.

Spikes Attacks:

Main Attack: Needle Grenade

Spike throws a cactus grenade which explodes when hitting something or when reaching its maximum range.
When it explodes it sends out 6 spikes that do damage. It is a 60 degree angle between each spike.
His cactus grenades explode into 6 spikes, which explode like this...
REMEMBER: NO MATTER WHICH ANGLE YOU THROW FROM, THE SPIKES ALWAYS MOVE IN THE SAME DIRECTION

Super: Stick Around!

Spike throws a grenade up(so it can be thrown over walls) and enemies inside the patch of spikes are slowed down and damaged over time.
The patch of spikes lasts for 5 secounds.

Spikes Gadget: Popping Pincushion

Spike shoots 3 waves of spikes in all directions. Each spike does 520 damage to enemies.
There are 10 spikes per wave and the spikes are not affected by Curveball.
He has 3 charges per match.

Spikes Star Powers:

Fertilize

When spike is inside his Super he regenerates 800 health per second.

Curveball

The spikes from his main attack(Needle grenade) now move in a curving motion, making it a bit easier to throw around walls
Remember: Spikes Gadget(Popping Pincushion) is not affected by Curveball
